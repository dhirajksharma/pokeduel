Poke Duel

Instructions

To play the game each player selects their unique pokemons from the ones provided. Each player then takes their turn to attack the other. The damage done is reflected in the progress bar above the pokemons. Each player will get 3 turns, post which the player with more power left wins!

To prevent unexpected problems, you must wait after selecting your pokemon, until it's name is revealed.
